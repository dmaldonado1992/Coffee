TYPES OF COFFEE

Espresso

A strong and concentrated coffee, usually served in a small shot, made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Americano

Espresso diluted with hot water, offering a less intense flavor but still rich in taste.

Cappuccino

Made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam, providing a balanced taste and creamy texture.

Latte

A creamy and smooth coffee, made with espresso and a larger amount of steamed milk, topped with a small layer of foam.

Macchiato

An espresso with a small amount of steamed milk or milk foam, offering a stronger coffee flavor with a light creamy touch.

Mocha

A combination of espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup, creating a rich, sweet, and indulgent flavor.
